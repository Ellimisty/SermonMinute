How to Play

▼
  1. Draw a card — Click the "Draw Card" button to reveal a Bible verse.
  2. Read the verse — Take a moment to read and reflect on the scripture shown.
  3. Start the timer — When you're ready, hit "Start Timer" to begin your 3-minute countdown.
  4. Preach! — Deliver a short sermon inspired by the verse. Speak from the heart!
  5. Need help? — Click the card to flip it over and see a few prompts to guide your sermon. These are optional!
  6. Pass the deck — When you're done, the next player draws a card and takes their turn.
